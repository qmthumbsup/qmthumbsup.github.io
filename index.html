Language of the politics in the UK

There is striking polarity between the current political speech, which seems to spark hatred, and the era of ‘political correctness’. In trying to understand this new wave of public language, researchers have focused on analysing the emotion invoked by politicians and the polarity of their rhetoric (3). Having these trends in mind, our group has decided to analyse political speeches in the UK by trying to find patterns in the vocabulary and related sentiment, according to their political wing.
